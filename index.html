About

I'm an independent researcher based in Morocco, focusing on secret sharing, verifiable secret sharing (VSS), and graph‑theoretic cryptography. I also teach high‑school mathematics and create educational content. My current work explores Pedersen VSS, matrix‑based schemes, and re‑randomization via group actions modeled on Cayley/Schreier graphs.

Keywords: Pedersen VSS, Feldman, DKG, expansion, spectral methods, Schreier graphs, algebraic coding, leakage resilience.
